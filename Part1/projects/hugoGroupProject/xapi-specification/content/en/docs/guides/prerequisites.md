Before the workshop, please make sure you have:

- A laptop with a modern browser
- A stable internet connection
- Access to the course site and repository
- Any workshop notes or credentials provided by the instructors

During the session, keep the documentation site open so you can follow the steps and record your results.

## Checklist

1. Confirm access to the workshop page.
2. Verify that links and media load correctly.
3. Test the tools you will use during the hands-on session.
