Hands-on session on the **xAPI** guides and **Twine** stories already published for this project. **Duration:** about 50–60 minutes.

![Workshop promotional image](hands-on-workshop/hero.png)

*Placeholder artwork (S1 candidate) until the group votes on the final motivational image.*

## Objectives

By the end of the workshop you should be able to:

- Explain what an xAPI statement is and why the LRS matters.
- Open the correct guide and Twine story for each theme.
- Send at least one statement through the Netlify proxy and confirm it in the test LRS.
- Spot common failures (bad IRI, wrong endpoint, CORS, LRS UI delay).

## Start here

1. [Prerequisites](/docs/guides/prerequisites/) — browser, connectivity, presentation PDF.
